A new place, different culture

Traveling, studying, working in different region. You might be face to a lot of unfamiliar norm and culture.

Shopping is also different

Different culture also exists in a shopping, like supermarket. When you try to buy fruit or vegetables, you could see “lb” unit.

What is lb,How much do will I pay?

By using “lb” unit, It is very difficult to grasp how much do you have to pay after all. Do you want to know how much it will cost if you buy one piece of apple ?

Survey

We conducted a survey to collect user's’ opinions. By asking several questions about supermarket unit and how how they think about “lb” unit, we were able to understand their desire.

Q: Have you ever felt any frustration, about the "lb" unit, when you are buying something?

Around 70% of the users experienced frustration or problem when they face to “lb” unit in the supermarket.

Q: Do you want to know how much it costs, if you buy one piece of food? For example, a price for one piece of apple.

90% of the users desire to know how much it will cost for each piece of food when they buy food.

Q: When you buy some fruits or vegetables, do you use a scale?

Even though, around 70% of the users don’t use scale in the supermarket when they buy fruit or vegetables.

Our product

A lot of people want to know the price per unit easily and quickly. So we created the best application which is called “For each item.” This application can calculate the price per unit of products.

How to use

1. Select the product

2. Set the price and size

3. Press "Calculate"

Coding

Two principles are mainly used for application product. The MVC (Model, View, Controller) and KISS (Keep It Simple Stupid) principles. These methods helped our understanding of the app's production much easier, faster, and cleaner. MVC model helped us planning and realizing our app. This was because this design pattern gave us an outline that how our ideas should be organized into actual code. Besides, MVC makes other developers easier to understand our code.
