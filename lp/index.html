KISS + MVC

We used two principles that help us create the app. They are MVC (Model, View, Controller) and KISS (Keep It Simple Stupid) principles. These methods helped our understanding of the app's production much easier, faster, and cleaner. MVC model helped us planning and realizing our app. This was because this design pattern gave us an outline that how our ideas should be organized into actual code. Besides, MVC makes other developers easier to understand our code.

Agile + Close communication

As for our communication tools, we used Slack and Notion. We mainly communicate through Slack. We also shared documents and mock-up files using google drive and Slack to make our product efficiently.

In terms of the meetings, we had group meetings every day throughout the week. We were able to have meetings regardless of time and location by using the google Meet service.

We created the to-do lists every day by using the Notion. Especially, we used the new timeline feature in Notion, which always allowed us to keep track of our progress and relevant conversations.
